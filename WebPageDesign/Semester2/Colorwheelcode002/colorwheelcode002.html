Color Wheel

The color wheel is composed of 12 colors. Three primary colors, three secondary colors, and six tartiary colors.

Primary Colors

  1. Red
  2. Green
  3. Yellow

Secondary Colors

Secondary colors are created by mixing two primary colors.

  1. Purple (red + blue)
  2. Green (blue + yellow)
  3. orange (yellow + red)

Tertiary Colors

Tertiary colors are created by mixing a primary and secondary color.

  1. Red-Orange
  2. Red-Purple
  3. Blue-Purple
  4. Blue-Green
  5. Yellow-Green
  6. Yellow-Orange
